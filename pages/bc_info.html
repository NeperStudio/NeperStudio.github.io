About

This application is a light and fast tool to convert values between bases.
Helpful for students learning to convert values to bases like binary and hexadecimal.
You can download this app through the link: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.neperstudio.baseconverter2to20
You can consult the app's privacy policy here.

How to use

Enter the bases of your interest in the fields on the left. Then write an expression in one of the fields on the right (on the basis indicated in the field on the left) and all conversions will be available in the remaining "expressions" fields.
Note: You can also edit any other "base" or "expression" field other than the initially edited field.

Notes

To use this app, Android 5.0 or higher is required.
